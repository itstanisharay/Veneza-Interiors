PROJECT OVERVIEW

Voyliq is a premium travel kit brand designed for modern travelers who value convenience without compromising on elegance. Voyliq is a premium travel kit brand designed for modern travelers who value convenience without compromising on elegance. Featuring a set of three minimalist liquid bottles, the brand needed an identity that reflected both functionality and sophistication, appealing to business and luxury travelers alike.

The Challenge
The Challenge

While the product itself was practical and well-designed, the brand lacked a compelling identity that differentiated it from standard travel kits. It needed a refined aesthetic and premium storytelling to attract a discerning audience.

While the product itself was practical and well-designed, the brand lacked a compelling identity that differentiated it from standard travel kits. It needed a refined aesthetic and premium storytelling to attract a discerning audience.

WHAT WE DID
WHAT WE DID

We developed a sleek and travel-inspired brand identity that highlights Voyliqs practicality and elegance. Using clean, minimal visuals and high-end packaging design, we positioned Voyliq as an essential companion for the sophisticated traveler. The result is a cohesive, upscale presentation that elevates the everyday travel experience.
